01

The From Zero to Hero Python Mentorship Program is a comprehensive 6-month virtual mentorship initiative designed to transform beginners and career switchers into confident, job-ready Python developers. The program combines structured classes, hands-on projects, personalized mentorship, and career guidance.

02

Yes, the program is entirely free for all selected participants. We are committed to making quality Python education accessible to aspiring developers across Africa and the world.

03

The program is open to beginners and intermediate learners. Whether you are a student, working professional, or career switcher with little to no prior coding experience, you are welcome to apply.

04

Yes, all mentors are experienced professionals who bring real-world knowledge and practical insights to support mentee growth.

05

The program runs for 6 months. Classes are held three times a week on Mondays, Wednesdays, and Saturdays from 8:00 PM to 10:00 PM WAT via Google Meet.

06

Each participant is placed in a small group of maximum 7 mentees with a dedicated mentor. Mentors provide personalized support, code reviews, and career guidance.

07

You will master Python fundamentals and progress to advanced topics including Data Structures, OOP, Data Analysis, Backend Development, and introductory ML and Cybersecurity.

08

Yes. Participants who successfully complete the program will receive a Certificate of Completion endorsed by From Zero to Hero and Black Python Dev.

09

All classes are recorded and made available to participants. However, consistent attendance is highly encouraged as the program is intensive.

10

We receive a high volume of applications. Selection is based on motivation, commitment, and potential impact.
