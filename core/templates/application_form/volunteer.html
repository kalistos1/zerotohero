"From Zero to Hero"
Volunteer Application

We are currently recruiting passionate industry professionals to join us as volunteers in the second cohort of the From Zero to Hero Python Mentorship Program. This is a unique opportunity to give back by mentoring the next generation of African Python developers.

Why you should volunteer

  • Make a real impact by mentoring underrepresented talents across Africa.
  • Gain leadership & mentoring experience valued by top tech companies.
  • Expand your professional network with industry experts and 200+ ambitious mentees.
  • Receive public recognition — certificates, LinkedIn features, and shout-outs.
  • Access to a strong talent pipeline for future hiring or collaboration.
  • Join a growing movement building the future of tech in Africa.
